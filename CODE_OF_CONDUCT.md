# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
PrintOrb community a friendly, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for
everyone — regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community. 🌍

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps build a positive environment:

- ✅ Being kind, patient, and respectful
- ✅ Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- ✅ Taking responsibility and apologizing when we get it wrong
- ✅ Focusing on what's best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- ❌ Insulting, derogatory, or demeaning comments and personal attacks
- ❌ Harassment of any kind, public or private
- ❌ Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- ❌ Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and
will take fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem
inappropriate.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at
**mfranke87@icloud.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly, respecting the privacy and security of the reporter.
